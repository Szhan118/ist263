Project Idea

My project will be an informational site on how to choose the best food for your dog. Since my concentration is on web design, I can show this website during my job interview or when I’m applying for internships as my ability of building websites. I personally own two dogs, and they each have their own customized dog foods according to their breed, size, activity level, and allergies. The main idea behind this project is that I hope creating this website can help some dog owners to find the right food for their dogs. Four pages I will have:

  1. Home page – describes what the website does
    • Content type: text, image
  2. Dry food page – rank of dry foods
    • Content type: text, image,links
  3. Wet food page – rank of wet foods
    • Content type: text, image,links
  4. Homemade recipe – 5min recipes that can be made at home
    • Content type: text, image,links

  • Link source: Chewy.com, petsmart.com, petco.com
