Home made dog food

There are many advantages of making your own dog food at home. And two major benefits are making your own dog food can be cheaper and safer for your dog. But making sure there are enough nutrition in the food is very important. Below are two receipes that you can make at home for your dog. You can also make these food in organic materials to boost up the nutritious level in your homemade dog food.

Homemade dog food for small breeds

  • Rice
  • Spinich
  • Carrots
  • Apples
  • Chicken thighs with skin
  • Chicken thighs without skin
  • Chicken gizzard
  • Chicken liver
  • Optional supplements
  1. Cooke the rice
  2. Chop up the spinich
  3. Chop up the carrots
  4. Remove seeds and chop up the apple
  5. Chop up all the chicken
  6. Cook the chicke until it changes color without oil and water
  7. Add in vegetables and apple when chicken changed color
  8. Optional salt in a very little amount
  9. Let cooked materials cool down and mix with Rice
  10. Optinal supplements add in to the meal
  11. Ready to serve!

Homemade dog food for large breeds

  1. Cook or steam or boil vegetables according to the receipe or your choice
  2. Cook and let cool the brown rice
  3. Smash the cooked vegetables with an uncooked whole egg (include the shell)
  4. Add in brown rice
  5. Mix all ingredients thoroughly
  6. Cut raw chickn into chunks
  7. Remove seeds and cut apple into chunks
  8. Mix chicken chunks with apple chunks
  9. Bake with oven
  10. Let cool and ready to serve!
